Learning Differences

Dyslexia
Literacy · Phonological Awareness

A learning difference that affects reading and related language-based processing skills, particularly phonological awareness.

arrow_forward
Dyscalculia
Numeracy · Logic

A specific learning disability that affects a person's ability to understand numbers and learn math facts.

arrow_forward
Dysgraphia
Writing · Fine Motor Skills

A learning disability that affects writing abilities. It can manifest as difficulties with spelling, poor handwriting and trouble putting thoughts on paper.

arrow_forward
Auditory Processing Disorder
Sensory · Communication

Affects how the brain processes auditory information, making it difficult to distinguish between similar sounds in words.

arrow_forward
Visual Processing Disorder
Sensory · Interpretation

Hinders the brain's ability to make sense of information taken in through the eyes, impacting reading and writing.

arrow_forward
