Meet Darcie Somers

With over fifteen years of dedicated experience in literacy education, Darcie Somers founded Hello Kids Therapy Hub with a single, unwavering mission: to ensure no child feels left behind in their academic journey.

Darcie's approach combines clinical precision with a compassionate understanding of child development. She believes that literacy is the master key — once a child learns to read and express themselves, they gain the agency to navigate any challenge the world presents.

Today, her clinic serves as a beacon for families seeking more than just tutoring; they seek a transformative educational home where every milestone is celebrated and every hurdle is seen as an opportunity for growth.
